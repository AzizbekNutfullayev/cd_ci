What is CDCI?

CDCI focuses on the intersection of creativity, digital innovation, and cultural expression. It includes fields like design, media, gaming, digital art, music production, and cultural heritage technologies.

CDCI Sectors

  • Film & Television
  • Video Game Development
  • Virtual Reality & Augmented Reality
  • Art & Cultural Technologies
